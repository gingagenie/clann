Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 April 2026

Clann ("we", "our", or "us") is a family organiser app for managing meals, tasks, and shopping lists. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: your email address and password (stored securely via Supabase Auth).
  • Household data: the name, tasks, meal plans, and shopping lists you create within the app.
  • Family members: first names you add to represent members of your household.
  • Push notification tokens: device tokens used to deliver reminders you have enabled.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and sync the Clann app across your household's devices.
  • To send push notifications for tasks and meal reminders you have opted into.
  • To allow household members to join using a shared household code.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your household data is only accessible to members of your household who have joined using your household code.

Push Notifications

If you enable reminders, we store a push subscription token on our servers to deliver notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in the app's Settings screen or your device settings.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email address below and we will process your request within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Clann is intended for use by adults managing a family household. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Children may be represented in the app by first name only, added by a parent or guardian.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at: hello@clann.app
